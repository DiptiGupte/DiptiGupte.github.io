Tacological | Virtual Reality Game

I collaborated with a group of three other students for a quarter long class project to create a virtual reality game that teaches players about food sustainability. We decided to make a taco themed game, where the player enters the virtual world of Tacolopolis and becomes the owner of a food truck. In the beginning of the round, they are able to choose between ingredients that are more sustainable or cheaper to build their taco. Once they have chosen their ingredients, they serve their tacos to customers with different ingredient preferences and sees the customer’s reactions to their tacos.



Maze | Virtual Reality Game

I worked on a quarter long class project to create a personal virtual reality game. I decided to make a technology themed maze. I used Maya to create the maze, where the walls look like circuits, and the objects that the player can collect to get more points. I then used Unity to create the game that allowed the player can move around the maze and shows the points that they currently have.



Saltwater Learning | Web App Game
